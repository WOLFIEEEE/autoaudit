## `char_key_shortcuts`

### `char-key-shortcut-accesskey`

- **Severity:** moderate
- **WCAG:** 2.1.4
- **Confidence:** medium
- **Source:** `audit/char_key_shortcuts.py:91`

**Sample title:** Single-character access key may violate Character Key Shortcuts

This element declares accesskey={...}, binding the character key {...} as a global shortcut. Single-character shortcuts fire regardless of focus and cannot be turned off by the user, which collides with screen-reader and speech-input pass-through keys. WCAG 2.1.4 (A) requires that such a shortcut can be turned off, remapped to include a modifier key, or be active only when its component has focus. Verify one of those mechanisms exists.

**Fix:** Provide a user setting to turn the access key off or remap it, require a modifier (Ctrl/Alt/Cmd) in addition to the character, or scope the shortcut to when the control is focused. Removing the accesskey is also acceptable when the shortcut isn't essential.
